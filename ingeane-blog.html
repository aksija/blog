MY BELOVED HOMETOWN : GILUTONGAN
by : Menguito, Ingeane A.

- Gilutongan Island, a pristine oasis nestled in the embrace of the Cordova Sea, stands as a testament to the unspoiled beauty that nature graciously bestows upon us. Gilutongan Island's greatest treasure lies beneath the surface of its crystal-clear waters. The island's charm extends beyond its natural allure to the warmth of its people and the flavors of its cuisine. Beyond its natural wonders, Gilutongan Island beckons travelers to connect with the local culture. Islanders, proud of their heritage, warmly welcome visitors to partake in traditional dances, share stories, and experience the rhythms of daily life.
