COOKING CHRONICLES : A SIMPLE LIFE IN THE KITCHEN

by : Inoc, Kim V.

- Cooking is a highly creative hobby to take up. You can create your own dishes, tweak well known recipes, blend your favourite cuisines, your creative juices will be in full flow. You can pick your own ingredients and do as you please, you can, mash, blend, boil, roast, dice, slice them, it's your choice. I love to cook because cooking has opened my eyes, heart, and stomach up to a lot of different foods that I thought I would never like or make. I never thought I would like Brussel sprouts, kale, and arugula , but I do! Cooking and food has motivated me to try different cuisines, foods, ingredients, and just be a little more opened and less picky. I love to cook is because it allows me to do things for others When I make a meal for others and see the satisfaction in their faces, it makes me feel delighted. Another reason I love to cook is because I love the different expressions my friends and family make when I ask them to taste my food.
