READING : A JOURNEY THAT NEVER ENDS

by : Bilangigue, Aiza Anita A.

Reading Wattpad has multiple benefits for users. It can help people improve their reading skills, increase their vocabulary, and expose them to new genres and authors. Wattpad also allows users to connect with other readers and writers around the world, share feedback, and discuss books. There are many aspects of Wattpad that are really good. One of those is that you get to read the novels as they are written. On Wattpad, you upload chapter by chapter. I like seeing how an author can create a story as they go, how they work with loopholes, how they fix those loopholes. And my favorite to read is about lovestory. We enjoy a good book because we feel what the protagonists is going through. We share his or her emotions and we try to help them somehow even when we know that is not possible. When i read i feel secure, capable of doing anything I want, I feel happy. When I read I forget all my worries and fears I just live the character life making his or her problems my own and trying to tell them how to act. Books inspire so many people and they make us dream with a better life because if they can why can't we?
